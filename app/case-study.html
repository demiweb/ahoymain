We built canacado.com with the understanding that Haskelberg nursery has 100+ different avocado rootstocks, and each complies with the different ground, soil, water, high, latitude & ect.

To help the user find the right rootstock for him we created 4 language website with a very unique tool. The ‘Find your Canacado” Quiz - a short survey that finds the right rootstock for the farmer. We created a Google search campaign with the most relevant keywords that avocado farmers will search like ‘Avocado phytophthora’, “Avocado illness & Rootstocks. In 4 different languages. Then we retargeted our audience on social media and lead the users to the quiz which generated great results.
